About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Centro de Investigación en Computación, Instituto Politécnico Nacional (IPN). My specialization is in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and my research interests are in Abusive Language Detection, Depression Detection, Emotion Detection and Fake News Detection. I have finished my thesis research work in "Context-Based Abusive Language Detection” under the supervision of Prof Alexander Gelbukh and Prof Arkaitz Zubaiga.

I have more than 4 years of professional experience as a Data scientist with extensive natural language processing, machine learning (ML), and project management skills. Love to turn vast amounts of text data into actions and organize teams around strategic goals.
